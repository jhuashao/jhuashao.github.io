    • 2017 - 2018 Rhode Island School of Design Viewbook

    “Our aim was to present a more comprehensive visualization of RISD's undergraduate programs oscillating between the voice of the institution and the voices of the members of the community itself. The publication contained a mixture of interviews, profiles, and info-graphics in addition to standard art-college admissions information.” - Micah Barrett (Creative Director, RISD Media)

    Over the summer I had the pleasure of interning at RISD Media, and working alongside a team of designers led by Micah in order to update and finalize the RISD Viewbook for 2017-2018.
