Just Love Here

Just Love Here is a typography and lettering exploration that explores multicultural identities. The lettering itself melds latin and hanzi characters, while retaining the meaning of each word they represent.

This project was featured in the -ISMs Exhibition on January 12th, 2017.

Poster Design / Digital Illustration

Just Love Here is an exploration of the concept of hyphenated Americans, and being of Chinese descent, I pursued a message that intertwined my two very unique cultures.

click to enlarge

The main typographic element that my graphic poster explores is the blending of cultures and mutual respect; not one dominating the other, but a harmonious balance between the two. Just Love Here is a call to understand, communicate, and respect.
