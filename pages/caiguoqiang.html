RISD Museum: Cai Guo Qiang

Designed for a fictional RISD Museum exhibition for internationally-acclaimed, Chinese fine artist, Cai Guo Qiang. Due to his immense popularity for western audiences, I wanted to pair Latin and Hanzi typefaces together to communicate to the two audiences he most frequently exhibits his work to.

Typefaces Used: GT America, and LanTingHei SC
