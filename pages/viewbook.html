2017 - 2018 RISD Undergraduate Viewbook

Working with Rhode Island School of Design’s Media Group, I had the pleasure of re-designing the Student Work section of the Undergraduate Viewbook. The Viewbook was originally designed by RISD Media’s Creative Director, Micah Barett. Our project was to refresh the book with new content and layouts, while simultaneously adhering to the original style guide.

The Viewbook is one of Media’s largest productions for the Admissions Department, with over 100,000 copies sent to prospective applicants and their respective schools. It is also made widely available for alumni, faculty, staff, and current students.
