Artist Ball in Wonderland

An animated poster I created as a proposal for a potential Artist Ball theme. This event is held every year and is extremely popular amongst RISD students. I created this poster to help promote the event, and used visual cues from the animated film in order to develop a palette and concept. The typography mimics the spiraling of the Cheshire cat, and has a sense of mystery to it.

Typeset entirely in GT Haptik and Druk
