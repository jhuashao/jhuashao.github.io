New York Times Design System

During the summer of 2018, I was hired as a Product Design Intern at The New York Times to help build and launch their first product design system.

I worked with my manager Andrei Kallaur, to finalize the work the previous intern, Julian Kelly, had started a year earlier. This project attempts to push towards developing a single source of truth across design and development teams (iOS, Android, Web) at The New York Times.
